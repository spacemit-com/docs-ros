# 1.6 Supported Hardware List

This section lists hardware devices tested with the Bianbu Robot SDK. These are **reference models** — you may use equivalent products as long as they meet the same specifications. Any special requirements are noted.

## Cameras

### MIPI Cameras
- [IMX219](https://e.tb.cn/h.hPIZv8PibRPGOTY?tk=6VfR43JgYzq)  
  • 15-pin, 1.0 mm pitch flexible flat cable (FFC)

- [OV5647](https://e.tb.cn/h.hPIY7HrB1rVBwQV?tk=UN3Z43JSDtS)  
  • 22-pin, 0.5 mm pitch flexible flat cable (FFC)

### USB Cameras
- Any USB camera supporting the **V4L2 standard** is compatible.  
- Example: [USB Camera (Reference Model)](https://e.tb.cn/h.hPYHwZZ9e1fFGQx?tk=LPhg43MexhS)

## Microphones

### USB Microphones
- Standard plug-and-play USB microphones are supported on all major operating systems.  
- Example: [USB Microphone (Reference Model)](https://e.tb.cn/h.6RQyI2nbCiB7fOX?tk=lSKQV1QIBk5)

### Circular Microphone Arrays
- Example: [Six-Microphone Circular Array](https://e.tb.cn/h.hPL0qG3FYUppkf0?tk=4VhL43MR8Fj)

## Speakers
- Recommended setup: small speaker paired with a USB plug-and-play sound card module.  
- Example: [Speaker + USB Sound Card (Reference Model)](https://e.tb.cn/h.6RQyI2nbCiB7fOX?tk=lSKQV1QIBk5)

## USB-to-Serial Modules
- Modules using **CH340** or **CH343** chipsets are supported.  
- Example: [CH340 / CH343 USB-to-Serial Module (Reference Model)](https://e.tb.cn/h.hPYJCPUjZFnyMoz?tk=e8gP43MXD5a)
