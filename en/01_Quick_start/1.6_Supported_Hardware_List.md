# 1.6 Supported Hardware List

This section lists hardware devices that are supported and recommended for use with the AI Robot SDK. The listed hardware serves as a reference or recommended selection; users may choose functionally equivalent alternatives based on their specific requirements. Any special specifications or requirements are noted in the corresponding entries.
